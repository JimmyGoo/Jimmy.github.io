Learning and Sharing

coding techniques and ideas

Design or Skill? Should a software engineer learn designing technique?

It is my first time to bulid my own web, and I have some feeling and ideas about the web front-end development during coding, which I really wanna share with you.

As a beginner, the first thing I learn in website coding is the basic programming rule of html and css. Concept like label, selector, class, link is fresh to me but it is easy to handle as I can learn this from the demo in online web-coding school such as w3school. After understanding the basic developing technique, I can't wait to build my first website and I put almost everything I know to make the web look better. It didn't cost a lot of time and I finish building the homepage in couple of hours. But as I use safari to open it, images and texts just bunched together and colors are mixing togother, making the whole page messy and terrible. Obviously and unfortunately, my first work failed and I realized I had to find out a good design pattern to make my website look comfortable and brilliant.

As a result, by using the google-the most efficient technique to learning coding skill, I found plenty of "perfect personal web design" and they were really fantastic. Those webs, decorated with bright color pattern as well as using smooth font with suitable size to show the text, looked clear and brilliant. It was a feast to surf them and you never felt boring with its perfect design. Thus, I try to use similar pattern to my own web. Although it can't be as outstanding as those "best website" I saw before, it was really pleasant to see my work become better. It is really important to learn both coding skill and designning technique to become a good front-end development engineer.

And I strongly recommend online website designing school like "canvas" to you, it helps you a lot!
